Traditional Indian Festivals

Diwali

Diwali, also known as the Festival of Lights, is celebrated by Hindus, Sikhs, and Jains. It symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil.

Holi

Holi is the Festival of Colors, marking the arrival of spring. It is celebrated with vibrant colors, joyous music, and dancing.

Eid-ul-Fitr

Eid-ul-Fitr marks the end of Ramadan, the Islamic holy month of fasting. It is a time for feasting, giving charity, and spending time with family.
